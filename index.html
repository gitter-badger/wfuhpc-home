About WFU HPC

The High Performance Computing Weifang University is led by Dr. Liqi Han. As an important public support platform for teaching and research in Weifang University, the center is based on high performance computing and is oriented to other disciplines and fields in the school, and actively carries out social service activities.

WFU HPC team particiates in student cluster competitions ASC, and achieved excellent honors.

WFU HPC is sponsored by Weifang University (and school of computer engineering).

History

ASC17
Won Highest LINPACK Award (breaking the world record with 31.7 TFLOPS/s, see ASC17 Finals). Reached the highest HPCG score with 922.333 GFLOPS/s, see Record Number of Conjugates Gradiented.
ASC16
First appearance in ASC. Won Prize of Excellence Award and ranked 22th overall the world.
